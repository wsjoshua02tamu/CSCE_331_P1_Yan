Service

I've been involved in community service ever since I joined the National Junior Honor Society in middle school; here are some selected service experiences I've had over the years.

Freestyle Underground Street Dancers Vice President (2021-Present): We are a club at Texas A&M University; we promote fun as well as the education about the Hip Hop dance culture in the Bryan/College Station area through various disciplines of freestyle dance, while improving members of the organization through large group workshops and small group teaching sessions. Follow us @TAMUFUSD!

  • Run the club social media to promote our organization and our events
  • Help teach workshops and guide our members with their dancing
  • Work with officers make decisions for our club, including budgeting, meeting planning, etc.
  • Apply for funding grants from the University and finding other fundraising opportunities
  • Event planning; duties include contacting outside talent, budgeting, promoting, and hosting
  • Planning and organizing various performances, including First Friday in Downtown Bryan, Howdy Week, and other opportunities

Community Youth Leadership Camp Assistant Counselor (2019): Residential summer camp for Asian youth in the DFW area

  • Helped supervise and mentor a group of nearly 40 middle school aged campers and helped lead them in programs that included recreation, dance and choreography, cultural activities, leadership lectures, and team building; involved extensive pre-camp preparation during summer

National Honor Society Member (2017-2020): nationwide high school merit based service organization

  • Involved for 3 years; volunteered for various different causes, including non-profits, private organizations, school events, and city events.

Chin Community Ministry (2017-2020): Non profit organization serving the Chin community, a persecuted Southeast Asian refugee group, in the DFW area
